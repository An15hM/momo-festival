About The Festival Mo:Mo Festival
London 2024

Indulge in a culinary journey to the heart of the Himalayas at London's Momo Festival. Discover an array of tantalizing flavors, from traditional to innovative takes on this beloved Nepalese dumpling. Join us for a celebration of culture, community, and mouthwatering delights. Don't miss out on this unforgettable gastronomic experience!

Happening On : 22nd July, 2024

Happening At: 139 Kings Road London WC24 5Z

Momo Madness

Dive into a world of momo varieties, from classic steamed to innovative fusion creations.

Live Cooking Demos

Witness skilled chefs craft momos right before your eyes, sharing their techniques and secret recipes.

Flavor Exploration

Embark on a journey of taste with curated tasting sessions, showcasing regional variations and unique ingredients used in momo preparation.

Tasting Tours

Embark on guided tasting tours to sample momos from different regions, discovering unique flavors and ingredients along the way.

Marketplace

Explore a bustling marketplace filled with vendors offering a diverse range of food, drinks, spices, and crafts inspired by Himalayan cuisine and culture.

Be the Judge

Attendees receive a digital platform or paper form where they can rate each vendor and their offerings based on taste, presentation, and overall experience.
